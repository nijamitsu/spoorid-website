FAQ

  1. What is SpoorID?

    SpoorID is a beautifully designed app created to help you identify animal scat with ease. It offers a clean interface where you can take a photo, get an instant analysis, and understand the wildlife around you with confidence.

  2. How do I analyze a scat sample?

    Tap the “+” button to open the camera preview. Snap a photo, and the app will begin the analysis automatically. When it finishes, a sheet will appear with the results. Tap “Save” to add it to your tracks, or choose “Cancel” to try another photo.

  3. Is my data private?

    Yes. All the birthday information you enter is stored securely and privately on your device. It is not sent to any external servers or cloud services.
